# Template: Follow-Up

**Use when:** No response to initial outreach after 5-7 days.

## Merge Fields
- `{{recipientName}}` — Person's name (or "there" if unknown)
- `{{orgName}}` — Their organization
- `{{senderName}}` — Your name
- `{{originalSubject}}` — Subject line of original email (for threading)
- `{{newAngle}}` — A new piece of context or value-add (optional)

---

## Follow-Up #1 (Day 5-7)

**Subject:** Re: {{originalSubject}}

Hey {{recipientName}},

Just bumping this up — I know inboxes are brutal. Would love to chat if the timing works.

{{newAngle}}

Either way, no worries. Just reply if interested.

{{senderName}}

---

## Follow-Up #2 (Day 14)

**Subject:** Re: {{originalSubject}}

Hey {{recipientName}},

Last nudge on this — totally understand if the timing isn't right. If anything changes down the road, I'm easy to find.

{{senderName}}

---

## Notes
- **Never send more than 2 follow-ups** to a cold outreach
- Keep follow-ups shorter than the original — respect their time
- If you have a `{{newAngle}}` (new project, new event, relevant news), include it in Follow-Up #1
- If you don't have a new angle, keep Follow-Up #1 very short (3 lines max)
- After Follow-Up #2 with no response, mark as "No Response" and move on
- **Threading:** Always reply to the original email thread, don't start a new one
