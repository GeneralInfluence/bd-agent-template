# Template: Cold Intro — General

**Use when:** Reaching out to a potential client or partner outside of a specific event context.

## Merge Fields
- `{{recipientName}}` — Person's name (or "there" if unknown)
- `{{orgName}}` — Their organization
- `{{senderName}}` — Your name
- `{{senderRole}}` — Your role
- `{{signal}}` — Why you're reaching out (something specific about their work)
- `{{relevantWork}}` — Brief example of RaidGuild work that's relevant to them

---

## Email

**Subject:** {{orgName}} + RaidGuild — potential fit?

Hey {{recipientName}},

I'm {{senderName}}, {{senderRole}} at RaidGuild — we're a decentralized collective of devs, designers, and project managers shipping Ethereum-native products.

I came across {{orgName}} and {{signal}}. We've done similar work — {{relevantWork}} — and I think there might be a fit.

Would you be open to a quick call to explore? No pressure, just a conversation.

Best,
{{senderName}}

---

## Notes
- The `{{relevantWork}}` should be a 5-10 word reference to a past RaidGuild project that's relevant
- Keep it under 100 words total — cold emails that are shorter get more replies
- If you don't have a specific person's name, "Hey there" is fine — don't use "Dear Sir/Madam"
