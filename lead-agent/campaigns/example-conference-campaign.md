---

## How to Use This Template

1. **Research** — Browse the speaker list and sponsor page. For each person of interest, note:
   - Their org and role
   - Why they might need your services (or be a good partner)
   - Mutual connections you could use for a warm intro

2. **Prioritize** — Tier leads into High / Medium / Low based on fit and signal

3. **Outreach** — Draft personalized messages using the conference-leads playbook
   - Mention the specific conference and what caught your attention
   - Reference their talk/work if applicable
   - Ask for a short meeting at the event or a call around it

4. **During the event** — Take notes in this file after each conversation
   - What did you learn?
   - Is this a real lead? What type?
   - What's the next step?

5. **After the event** — Add confirmed leads to the pipeline via the BD Agent
